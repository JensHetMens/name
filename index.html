  • About Me

    Hey! I'm Jens van der Sloot, a 16 year old that has a passion for websites.

    When I started programming, I was not quite sure what to focus on, so I started with the basics: Python, JS, and C++. Although I really enjoyed working with these languages, I felt like something was missing. When I started web design about a year and a half ago, that feeling disappeared in an instant. Here I was able to express all my creativity not only in design, but in so many other ways as well! Over the past few years I have gained a lot of experience in HTML, CSS and JS, but I am still very eager to learn new things!
